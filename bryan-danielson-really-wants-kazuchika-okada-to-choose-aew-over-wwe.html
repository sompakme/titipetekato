Bryan Danielson Really Wants Kazuchika Okada to Choose AEW Over WWE

 Posted on July 20, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  203 words  |  Martina Birk

Bryan Danielson has many people he has shared the ring with and that especially includes Kazuchika Okada. With Okada set to head to the USA for the next stage of his career, Bryan Danielson made it clear he wants Okada in AEW over WWE.

Kazuchika Okada finally parted ways with New Japan Pro Wrestling on February 1st, bringing an end to his 17-year-long run with the promotion. Obviously, fans still wonder if he will choose AEW or WWE even now.

In a recent Tokyo Sports interview, AEW’s Bryan Danielson discussed Okada’s upcoming free agency. The American Dragon made it clear that he wants to see The Rainmaker in AEW over WWE.

“Of course I want him to come to AEW. Fans all over the world know how great Okada is. Both WWE and AEW are major players, but I hope he comes to AEW.”

It was previously reported that Kazuchika Okada is set to join AEW after he is done with his NJPW commitments. It remains to be seen how he will be booked once he is finally in AEW.
