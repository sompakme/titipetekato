FAQs

Does Vineyard Vines have free shipping?

Get free shipping on your Vineyard Vines order by spending $125 or more. If your order is less than this, standard shipping will be calculated at the checkout. You can also choose expedited shipping if you prefer.  

How can I get a Vineyard Vines welcome offer?

When you sign up for the Vineyard Vines newsletter, you can get a 10% off promo code for your first purchase. To sign up, click on our relevant Vineyard Vines coupon or go to the Vineyard Vines website footer. You will need to give your email address to be sent your unique code. 

Can I get a student discount at Vineyard Vines?

Yes, any college or graduate students can get a 15% off promo code at Vineyard Vines. Simply verify your student status via SheerID to be sent your one-time Vineyard Vines promo code. After 30 days, you can ask for another discount code.  

Where can I get Vineyard Vines for sale?

Shop the Vineyard Vines sales all year round with the dedicated ‘sale’ page on the brand’s website. Our team has previously noted ‘sale on sale’ where you can get an extra 40% off sale items already discounted. Make sure to check this page for regular sale updates. 

What is the Vineyard Vines returns policy?

Returns can be made to Vineyard Vines within 60 days for a complete refund. For sale items, this is 30 days. Your items should be returned unused, new, and with any tags still attached.  Returns after 60 days will be refunded via store credit. Sale items returned after 60 days or full-price items after 90 days can only be refunded for a store credit of the latest retail price.
