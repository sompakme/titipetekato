BRITTANY Mahomes dazzled at the 2023 Kentucky Derby as she posed for pictures with her husband and Kansas City Chiefs quarterback Patrick.

Elsewhere at Churchill Downs, Las Vegas Raiders star Davante Adams showed up in an interesting company, reuniting with Aaron Rodgers and other ex-Green Bay Packers teammates.

Sports athletes and their partners looked the part over the weekend, showing off all sorts of eye-catching looks at the 149th Kentucky Derby.

The Mahomeses led the way, proudly documenting their arrival at the first of the Triple Crown races on social media.

In the days leading up to the big event, the organizers announced that Mahomes would make the iconic Riders Up call before the start of the main race on Saturday.

And so the couple made sure they looked stunning before the two-time NFL MVP got to deliver the famous command.

Brittany wowed in a shiny pink dress on Derby Eve when the annual Barnstable Brown gala took place.

She then donned a violet dress with a matching handbag and pink hat as Patrick went for blue pants and a light checkered jacket over a blue shirt on Saturday.

Meanwhile, Raiders wide receiver Adams uploaded snaps of himself smoking cigars with ex-Packers teammate Allen Lazard.

The two NFL stars were part of a bigger Green Bay reunion at this year's Derby.

Randall Cobb told sportscaster Kay Adams that he, Lazard, Rodgers, David Bakhtiari, AJ Hawk, Bryan Bulaga, and Jimmy Graham all had a chance to hang out with Davante.

They immortalized the occasion by posing as the entire "pack" for a picture together.

Buffalo Bills quarterback Josh Allen also made a splash, raising eyebrows with his peach-and-vanilla jacket before changing to a more neutral light green suit.

Meanwhile, Kay Adams distracted fans by donning a lingerie-like dress as she reported from Churchill Downs on Friday.

And, FOX Sports host Joy Taylor stunned in an elegant black dress on the red carpet.
