What is the face off sex position?

The face off sex position basically does what it says on the tin - as two partners literally get face to face to get down to it.

The position is credited with making partners feel more connected, due to the intimate nature of their faces being so close, and the eye contact that comes along with it.

How to do the face off sex position

To enjoy the face off position, the penetrating partner sits at the edge of a bed, chair, sofa - or wherever feels comfortable.

They should plant their feet on the floor for support and have their legs slightly apart.

The receiving partner then straddles them, ending up face to face, and wraps their legs around their partner's body.

The partner on top can be in control of the angle and depth of the penetration.

The position also provides a lot of support thanks to both partners being sat down, so it should be comfortable to stay in the face off for a while.
