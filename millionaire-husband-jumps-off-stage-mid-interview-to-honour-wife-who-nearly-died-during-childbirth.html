Papichulo revealed that he and his wife came from poverty together to make $20M+ and created over 50 millionaires.

"Ain’t nothing more important to me than loving life with the woman that gave me life when we were in low-income housing…My wife @lady_livii and I have been through so much this year…"The fact that I get to roll over and see her and my daughter's faces as they sleep is another reason for me to get up and grind The love of great women created this 8figure earner, Best selling author, philanthropist, etc I wouldn’t be here without her. Share Not just Black Love but Love in its purest Form."
