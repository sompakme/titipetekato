Kylie Minogue Age: How Old Is Kylie Minogue?

Kylie Minogue was born on May 28, 1968. She was born in Melbourne, Australia.

Kylie Minogue Parents 

Kylie Minogue was born to Carol Jones and Ronald Charles Minogue. Her mother, Carol, is a former ballet dancer. Her father is a fifth-generation Australian, and has Irish ancestry.

Kylie Minogue Siblings 

Kylie Minogue has two siblings. Her siblings are Dannii Minogue and Brendan Minogue. Wikipedia describes Dannii as Danielle Jane “Dannii” Minogue is an Australian singer, television personality and actress. Born and raised in Melbourne, Minogue first gained recognition for her appearances on the television show Young Talent Time and for her role as Emma Jackson on the soap opera Home and Away. Brendan is also described by Wikipedia as Brendan Minogue was born on 30 August 1970 in Melbourne, Victoria, Australia. He is married to Rebecca. They have one child. 

Kylie Minogue Husband 

Kylie Minogue has never been married. According to records on her relationships, she was recently engaged to British actor Joshua Sasse. They were engaged in 2016 but broke up in 2021.

Kylie Minogue Children 

Kylie Minogue has never had children. She has revealed in an interview in the past that breast cancer prevented her from having children of her own.
