    They are one of Britain's golden couples – but that didn't stop Victoria Beckham jokingly ask magician David Blaine to make her husband vanish on this week. David Beckham was the special celebrity guest on the ABC TV show on Tuesday, but it was wife Victoria who delivered the best line of the night, despite being off-camera and in another room.

    "How is that even possible," awestruck David exclaimed after watching the American magician in action, to which his wife responded with perfect timing: "Has anyone made David disappear yet?!"

    David Beckham was visibly shocked after watching David Blaine in action

    The former footballer was visibly shocked by the trick, which saw the illusionist sew his mouth shut before spitting out two live frogs.

    "He says name an animal he can keep alive in his hand. I said a frog," David explains. "So he sewed his mouth shut with a needle and thread. I thought the sewing the mouth was the trick, forgot about the frog bit…

    "Live frogs came out of his mouth!!" he then exclaims, holding his head in his hands. "Oh my God!!"

    Son Brooklyn Beckham also appears in the footage

    David isn't the only celebrity to star in the new series of . Johnny Depp, Arnold Schwarzenegger, Jennifer Lawrence, John Travolta and Emma Stone have also been left astounded by the 43-year-old magician.

    The show, which will be David's final TV special before he sets off on his first world tour, has been three years in the making.

    "The first part was learning how to do all sorts of new things that I've never done before. Things that were historical pieces of magic. Things that were legendary stories in magic where the actual performers took their secrets to the grave," he told .

    The star holds his head in his hands as he tries to make sense of the illusion

    "You see the progress of learning these for the first time. You'll see behind the scenes and how I work on the magic effect and then you see me go out into the real world and use these new pieces of magic. I look for amazing and truthful responses from people of all walks of life."

    David added: "This is my favourite special so far. The fact that it's the first time that there's an actual story and a little bit of a reveal other than just me doing the magic as a mysterious strange character — you get a peek inside of my world.
