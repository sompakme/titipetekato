Michael Clifford is an Australian guitarist, musician, and social media star. He is well-known as the lead guitarist, face, and voice of the music band “5SOS” (5 Seasons of Summer) along with Luke Hemmings, Calum Hood, and Ashton Irwin.

Early Life and Career

  • Michael Clifford was born on November 20, 1995, in Sydney, Australia.
  • He attended Norwest Christian College but dropped out to pursue his music career.
  • Clifford met Luke Hemmings and Calum Hood at school, and they started composing music together.
  • They gained popularity by uploading music videos and cover songs to their YouTube channel, “5 Seasons of Summer,” which has over 5.7 million subscribers.
  • In 2011, Ashton Irwin joined the group, and they formed the music band “5SOS.”
  • Their music video caught the attention of Louis Tomlinson from “One Direction,” who shared a link to their video on his social media account.
  • In 2014, they released their self-titled debut album.
