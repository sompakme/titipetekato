Nigerian dancer Korra Obidi and husband welcome their first child

 Posted on July 14, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  343 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

- Popular Nigerian dancer, Korra Obidi, and her American husband, Justin Dean, are currently celebrating

- The couple has finally witnessed the birth of their first child

- Korra and Justin announced on social media that their daughter, June Chukwuebube Dean, was born on July 9, 2019

The feeling one gets when they finally welcome their own child is indescribable but it often borders on joy and excitement.

Just recently, popular Nigerian dancer Korra Obidi and her American husband, Justin Dean, welcomed the birth of their first child.
