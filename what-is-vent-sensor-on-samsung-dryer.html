What is the purpose of the vent sensor in a Samsung dryer?

When a blockage in the exhaust vent is detected by the dryer, a Vent Check Sensor symbol is shown on certain models to alert the user of the situation. When the ductwork is more than 80% obstructed or clogged, the symbol will flash for 3 hours after a cycle has been finished to indicate this.
