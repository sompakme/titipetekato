Early Life And Education

Patrick Duffy was born on March 7, 1949, in Townsend, a small town located in Montana. His parents, Terrence and Marie Duffy, owned local taverns. Patrick also has a sister named. 

Joanne Hunt. During his academic years, he attended Cascade High School and later studied at the prestigious University of Washington.

Career

Duffy began his professional journey by taking on smaller parts in many television shows and movies. However, it wasn’t until 1978 that he experienced a breakthrough that would change the course of his career forever. 

This pivotal moment arrived when he secured the coveted role of Bobby Ewing in the highly popular primetime soap opera Dallas. The character of Bobby Ewing became synonymous with Duffy, catapulting him into widespread fame and gaining him immense acclaim on a global scale.

Duffy portrayed Bobby Ewing in Dallas until 1985. During that time, his character met a notorious demise in a shower scene. However, to everyone’s astonishment, the writers ingeniously resurrected Bobby in 1986 by unveiling that the preceding season was merely a dream.

 This unexpected turn of events became one of television’s most highly acclaimed moments. Duffy’s television career flourished as he appeared in multiple shows and movies. Additionally, he explored directing and taking charge of episodes for Step by Step, Dallas, and Major Crimes. 

After branching out, Duffy returned to his soap opera beginnings in 2006, joining The Bold and the Beautiful as Stephen Logan. He portrayed the character until 2011 and made subsequent returns in 2022 and 2023.

Duffy returned as Bobby Ewing in the Dallas revival series, which aired from 2012 to 2014. Since then, he has engaged in entertainment by taking on different guest roles and participating in various projects.

Personal Life

Duffy, 75, marriage to ballerina Carlyn Rosser lasted an impressive 43 years, beginning in 1974 and sadly ending with her passing in 2017. They were blessed with two sons, Padraic and Conor, throughout their union. The bond between Duffy and Carlyn brought immense joy and a sense of security into his life.

 He has even labelled her as his “soulmate,” acknowledging her profound impact on him. Carlyn first introduced Duffy to Buddhism, a belief system that has since become integral to his existence.
