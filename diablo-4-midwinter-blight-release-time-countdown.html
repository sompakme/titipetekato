First Published December 6, 2023, 19:40

Attention, heroes of Sanctuary! Diablo 4's first-ever seasonal event is releasing this December. It is called Midwinter Blight, and we are all very excited to slay the red-cloaked terrors that lurk in the Fractured Peaks.

If you are as hyped as we are, especially with Season of Blood turning it around for Blizzard's latest ARPG, then we've got you covered. In this guide, we share the exact release time of Midwinter Blight alongside a countdown timer that you can stare at...

Update on 6 December 2023: We've updated this article with the latest info and exact timing of Diablo 4's Midwinter Blight holiday event.

Diablo 4 Midwinter Blight Release Time & Date

Players will start their quest to slay the red-cloaked terrors in Kyovashad, Diablo 4's main city hub in the Fractured Peaks region, where the event takes place. The Midwinter Blight event starts on 12 December 2023, following a server maintenance period. 

Therefore, we expect the exact release time for Diablo 4's Midwinter Blight event to be 19:00 CEST in Europe and 09:00 PDT in North America, so mark your calendars and set those alarms. 

Get hyped with an official lore description of Midwinter Blight to get the blood flowing:

Ever wonder what the holidays are like in Sanctuary? Well, now you can find out in our first seasonal event, Midwinter Blight, beginning on December 12 and lasting for three weeks. Trudge through the snow-laden, winter terror-land of Fractured Peaks, and be sure to keep your eyes peeled for a mysterious, red-cloaked terror!

Midwinter Blight will run for three (3) weeks, until 2 January 2024. We expect the event to end at the start of the maintenance period, so 18:00 CEST on European servers and 08:00 on US servers.
