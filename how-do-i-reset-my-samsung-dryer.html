You may reset the EEPROM in your dryer model by unplugging it from the power source for about 10 minutes. Reconnect the dryer and hold down the Wrinkle Prevent and Mixed Load Bell buttons at the same time for 3 seconds after it has been unplugged for 10 minutes.

Does a dryer, on the other hand, have a reset button?

Many dryers include a reset button on the control panel, which may be used to restart the machine. Allow the dryer to cool for about 10 minutes if the motor would not start. After that, press the reset button. If there are no issues with the motor, switches, or electrical system, this should be sufficient to restart the drying machine.

What is causing my dryer to not start all of a sudden?

In most dryers, the drive motor rotates both the drum and the blower wheel, which exhausts the air through the dryer. If the dryer will not start, it is possible that the motor is faulty. Check the thermal fuse, the start switch, and the door switch before replacing the motor because all three are more likely to be the source of the problem. It is not possible to test the dryer motor easily.
