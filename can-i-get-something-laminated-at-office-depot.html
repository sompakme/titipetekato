How can I laminate paper at a low cost?

The simplest and most inexpensive method of laminating your documents is to cut several strips of broad packing tape and gently arrange the material on top of the document. Make certain that the packing tape extends beyond the edge of the paper by about 14 inches. Then, flip the page over and attach pieces of packing tape to the opposite side of the document.

What is the best way to repair a laminated card?

Place the card in between two layers of a brown paper bag and then run the iron over it for 3-4 seconds to seal the edges of the paper. Examine the paper to check whether the lamination has melted to the surface. If this is the case, go through it again.
