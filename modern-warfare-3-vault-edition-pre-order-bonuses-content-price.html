Modern Warfare 3 Vault Edition: Pre-Order Bonuses, Content, Price

 Posted on September 3, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  385 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Looking to grab the Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 3 Vault Edition? Here's everything we know so far about what's coming with this edition.

First Published August 21, 2023, 13:51

Excitement is ramping up for Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 3 as Activision is steadily revealing more information on the upcoming title. As a direct sequel to last year's Modern Warfare 2 (2022), this upcoming release marks the first time Call of Duty has received a back-to-back sequel. Players will once again team up with Captain Price, Ghost, and more in order to take on new threats and dangerous missions.

Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 3 is set to launch on November 10, 2023, and players are keen to get their pre-orders in. While players will be able to get their hands on Standard Editions in both Physical and Digital form, the more dedicated fans will be eager to pre-purchase the Vault Edition.

Much like with past CoD titles, pre-ordering the Vault Edition will offer various bonuses and Early Access periods to ensure players start off MW3 with plenty of treats. Below, we'll go over everything that we know so far about the upcoming Vault Edition for Modern Warfare 3.

Table of Contents

Call of Duty: MW3 Vault Edition: All Bonus Content, Rewards

Activision have confirmed all of the bonus coming in the MW3 Vault Edition, which is set to include the following:

  • Nemesis Operator Pack: 4 Operator Skins (Price, Ghost, Warden, and Makarov)
  • Soap Operator Pack - immediate use in MW2 & Warzone, and at launch for MW3
  • 2 Weapon Vaults
  • BlackCell (1 Season) + bonus tier skips: includes Battle Pass, 1100 CP, 50 Tier Skips, and more
  • Early Access to MW3 Open Beta
  • Up to a week Early Access to MW3 Campaign

Call of Duty: MW3 Vault Edition Price

The MW3 Vault Edition will set players back $99/£99, similar to that of the MW2 Vault Edition. While this might seem steep, considering the two Operator Packs and BlackCell Season it comes with, the price begins to make more sense.

Call of Duty: MW3 Vault Edition: How To Pre-Order

Players can pre-order the MW3 Vault Edition via Activision's official Call of Duty store across all platforms: PlayStation, Xbox & PC. In addition, the Vault Edition will also be available to pre-purchase through each platform's digital storefronts.
