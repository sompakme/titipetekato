2013 BET Hip Hop Awards Performances

 Posted on September 30, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  235 words  |  Aldo Pusey

 A few weeks ago some of the biggest names in Hip Hop took to Atlanta, Georgia for the 2013 BET Hip Hop Awards. Tonight, we get to see what all went down that night.

Many of the game's biggest stars were not only just there in attendance, but were taking their talents to the stage. Performers at this year's award show included an all-star line up featuring Meek Mill, Future, Rick Ross, Schoolboy Q, Diddy, 2 Chainz, Kendrick Lamar, French Montana, DJ Khaled, Snoop Dogg and the list goes on. Oh yeah, Let's not forget about the always memorable cyphers that go down as well. Expect to hear cyphers from the Slaughterhouse crew, TDE crew, ASAP Mob, and more! Another great night in Hip Hop.
