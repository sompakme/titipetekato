How to Shop Uniqlo x Sofia Coppolaa Film Buff's Fashion Dream

 Posted on September 21, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  311 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

You can tell a lot about a person by what their favorite film is, and this is especially so when it comes to the cinematic universe of Sofia Coppola. The Bling Ring is for those who crave early aughts debauchery and drama, while Marie Antoinette is for fashion devotees (and fans of The Strokes). The list goes on.

Now, Coppola ultra-fans can showcase their admiration for the Academy Award-winning director with a new graphic tee collection courtesy of Uniqlo. The six-piece collaboration features two timeless wardrobe staples—the plain white tee and the canvas tote bag— embellished with quotes and memorable scenes from some of the director's most beloved films including The Virgin Suicides, Lost in Translation, and more.

“I love seeing people responding to my work after all these years. Especially Virgin Suicides and Marie Antoinette which weren’t seen much when they came out," Coppola tells BAZAAR of the new collection. "I’m happy that they seem to be inspiring another generation and living on! It’s always nice to feel connected to others [in that way]."

Coppola, known for her smart personal aesthetic in addition to her cult-classic-filled film catalogue, isn't afraid to sport the collection in her own way, too.

"I wear mine with a Chanel jacket or cardigan and love seeing them layered over a long sleeve striped t-shirt," she adds. "Or however anyone feels like! I love seeing how different people wear them.”

The 'Celebrating Sofia Coppola UT Collection' is available to shop now on Uniqlo's website. Act fast and pick the piece for your favorite Coppola film below.
