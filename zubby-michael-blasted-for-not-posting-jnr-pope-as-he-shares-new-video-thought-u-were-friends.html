  • It all started when Zubby shared a video of himself appearing to be chilling few days after the tragic deaths that rocked Nollywood
  • A number of Nigerians trooped to the actor’s comment section to express their dismay as they blasted him

Popular Nollywood actor Zubby Michael was recently tackled by netizens following his social media post after his colleague Jnr Pope’s death.

Recall that April 10, 2024 was a sad day for Nollywood when actor Jnr Pope and four other crew members drowned in Anam River, Anambra, after their boat capsized while returning from a movie location.

A few days after the tragic incident, Zubby took to his Instagram page to share a video of himself appearing to be chilling with someone.

Video of unconscious Jnr Pope's wife trends

Meanwhile, Legit.ng earlier reported that after Jnr Pope Odonwodo's death, details about how his young wife, Qute Jay, was faring have made the rounds online.

Two days after Jnr Pope’s tragic demise, a Nigerian nurse took to TikTok to share a video of the actor’s widow at the hospital.

The nurse on TikTok then went on to reveal that the mother of three was unconscious as a result of her husband’s death.
