Shoppers Say This Mask From an Oprah-Approved Brand Firms Skin

 Posted on September 4, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  466 words  |  Aldo Pusey

My winter skin is bumming me out. It’s the embodiment of womp womp — congested, dull, and flaky. The thing is I haven’t changed anything in my skincare routine to account for this, it’s simply that my skin needs an extra boost during these cold dry months. If your skin is similarly revolting against you, let me suggest something: Cycle in a weekly treatment. More specifically, cycle in Dermalogica’s Multivitamin Power Recovery Mask. 

If you’ve tried Dermalogica products before, you know you’re in for a product that delivers on its promises. If you haven’t, the brand is Oprah-approved and used by celebrities like Courteney Cox. Multivitamin Power Recovery Mask is a multi-tasking powerhouse that may in fact be your (and my) winter skin savior. A 10-15 minute session with this mask purports to yield results that include increased firmness, brightness, and glow. 

There is a CVS receipt-long list of effective and efficient ingredients here, but the highlights are vitamin A (retinol), vitamin C, and linoleic acid. Retinol diminishes wrinkles, clears skin, and evens out both texture and discoloration. Vitamin C is another popular superstar ingredient that protects your skin from elements that cause signs of aging, improves hyperpigmentation, and encourages more supple, firm skin. Linoleic acid is anti-inflammatory, moisturizing, moisture-retaining, and has soothing properties. It’s a nice counterbalance to Multivitamin Power Recovery Mask’s more intense active ingredients. 

Science lesson aside, does the mask actually work? The 700-plus five-star reviewers say yes. A shopper in their 60s wrote, “my skin appears smoother and more even-toned, hydrated and refreshed.” They added that when they apply it at night they wake up with a “plump and glowing” complexion in the morning.

Skin Resurfacing Lactic Acid Cleanser

All face washes are the same until one isn’t. More than 900 reviewers would probably tell you that’s the case with this Lactic Acid Cleanser. A shopper in their 40s called Dermalogica’s cleanser “a game changer” and said they “saw a difference with [their] first wash.” It made their skin “clean, smooth, and bright.”
