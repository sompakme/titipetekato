Why do Italians say salute when they toast?

If you would like to make a toast, you would say “fare un brindisi”. Italians love to say “cin cin” because it recalls the sound of glass touching when making the toast. “Salute” is the more formal way of making a toast and, similar to other languages, it literally means to your health.

What do you say when you toast in Italy?

How do you toast in Italian? If you'd like to make a toast, you can either say "cin cin" or "salute". "Cin cin" recalls the sound the glasses make when clinked, while "salute" literally means health.
