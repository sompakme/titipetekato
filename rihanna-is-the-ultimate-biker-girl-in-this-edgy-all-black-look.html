Rihanna Is the Ultimate Biker Girl in This Edgy All-Black Look

 Posted on August 21, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  349 words  |  Martina Birk

Rihanna attended the Formula 1 Heineken Silver Las Vegas Grand Prix looking like the ultimate biker It girl Saturday night.

David Becker - Formula 1

The “Diamonds” singer stepped out in a monochromatic black outfit composed of opaque satin tights by Balenciaga, a zip-up hoodie that she kept low and plunging—exposing her black bra—and an oversized leather jacket with white accents. She finished the look with black pointed-toe pumps, dramatic black racing sunglasses, choker and pearl necklaces by Messika, diamond stud earrings, and a silver anklet on her left ankle. Rihanna wore her honey-blonde locks down in soft waves and sweeping across her forehead, and rounded things off with a bold red lip.

Walking alongside her was A$AP Rocky, who celebrated the debut of his F1 x Puma collection the night before. He gave a casual outfit some fun accents, wearing dark wash jeans, a white tee, and a blue pinstripe button-down unbuttoned, along with gray driving gloves that featured a multi-colored heat map from his collection, orange work shoes, and a pearl choker.

Dan Istitene - Formula 1

The event saw a lot of star power, with the likes of Cara Delevigne, Heidi Klum, Brad Pitt, Paris Hilton, and David Beckham in attendance.

Rihanna has been leaning into her edginess for the racing weekend. On Friday night, she was the perfect balance of glam and cool in a dark brown leather patchwork style jacket, black open-toe pumps, diamond stud earrings by De Beers, a collection of sparkly silver necklaces, and the same silver anklet. Earlier that day, while catching her flight to Las Vegas, she sported Michael Jordan’s red-and-black Chicago Bulls jersey, a black satin bomber jacket, a black maxi cargo skirt with a center slit, and a red and white trucker hat from A$AP Rocky’s AWGE. She showed some love to her own Puma line, pairing the look with her Fenty Puma Creepers.
