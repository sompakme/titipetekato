Amelia Vega is a model, actress, author, singer, and Miss Dominica pageant winner. And the wife of NBA star Al Horford. With her victory in the 2003 Miss Universe contest, she became the first Dominican in history to be crowned Miss Universe.

Vega was born on November 7, 1984 in Santo Domingo. Dominican Republic. Her father, Otto Miguel Vega Rasc (Professional Doctor) was the son of a lawyer and a university professor. Her mother, Patricia Polanco Alvarez, was Miss Dominican Republic at the 1980 Miss World pageant.
