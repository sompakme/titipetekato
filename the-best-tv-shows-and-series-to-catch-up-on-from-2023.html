The Last of Us

HBO/Warner Media

Video game adaptations are not normally as revered and critically acclaimed as The Last of Us. That is perhaps because this game has not been made for the screen solely for jump scares, action, and violence (though there is plenty of that), but has instead been given the serious, brooding and smart treatment typically reserved for a serious HBO drama. Which is, of course, what this is. Featuring blisteringly good central performances from Pedro Pascal and Bella Ramsey, this is an emotional rollercoaster of beautifully rendered human moments amid extraordinary circumstances.
