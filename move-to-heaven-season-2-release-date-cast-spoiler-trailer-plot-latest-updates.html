Move To Heaven Season 2: Move To Heaven is a South Korean drama series that premiered on Netflix in May 2021. The series is based on the essay “Things Left Behind” by Kim Sae-byul, a former trauma cleaner who shared his experiences of cleaning up the belongings of the deceased and delivering their final messages to their loved ones. The show follows Geu-ru, a young man with Asperger’s syndrome, who inherits his father’s trauma cleaning business and works with his uncle Sang-gu, a former boxer and ex-convict. Together, they encounter various stories of life and death as they move the hearts of the people they meet.

The first season of Move To Heaven received critical acclaim and positive reviews from viewers around the world. The show was praised for its heartwarming and emotional storytelling, its realistic and sensitive portrayal of trauma cleaning, its diverse and complex characters, and its stellar performances by the cast. The show also touched on various social issues such as domestic violence, suicide, organ donation, LGBTQ+ rights, and disability awareness.

Many fans of the show are eagerly waiting for the second season of Move To Heaven, hoping to see more of Geu-ru and Sang-gu’s journey and the stories of the people they help.
