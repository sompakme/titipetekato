Nigerian music superstar Burna Boy received wide criticism after he completely shaved off his trademark beards.

Many fans opined that he doesn't look cool with his beards off and he has responded to their comments via a post on his Instagram story.

The self-acclaimed African Giant threatened to even cut the hairs on his head if that was all fans loved him for.
