Bernie Taupin’s Early Life and Career

Bernie Taupin was born in Lincolnshire, England, and developed a passion for music and poetry at a young age. His career took off when he responded to an advertisement by Liberty Records seeking new songwriters. This led to his fruitful partnership with Elton John, which has lasted for over five decades and produced numerous chart-topping hits.

Collaboration with Elton John

Taupin’s collaboration with Elton John has been one of the most successful partnerships in music history. Their combined talents have resulted in over 30 albums, with Taupin penning the lyrics for most of John’s biggest hits, including “Rocket Man,” “Tiny Dancer,” and “Candle in the Wind.” This partnership has significantly contributed to Taupin’s wealth.

Solo Projects and Other Collaborations

Apart from his work with Elton John, Taupin has also embarked on solo projects and collaborated with other artists. He has released several solo albums and written lyrics for artists like Starship, Heart, and Alice Cooper. These ventures have also added to his net worth.

Income from Royalties

As a lyricist, Taupin earns a significant portion of his income from royalties. Every time a song he has written is played, sold, or streamed, he receives a percentage of the revenue. With the enduring popularity of his songs, this is a substantial source of income.

Real Estate Investments

Taupin has made savvy investments in real estate over the years. He owns several properties in the United States, including a ranch in Santa Ynez Valley, California. The value of these properties contributes to his overall net worth.

Awards and Recognition

Taupin’s talent as a lyricist has earned him numerous awards and recognition. He has won multiple Grammy Awards and was inducted into the Songwriters Hall of Fame in 1992. In 2020, he won the Academy Award for Best Original Song for “(I’m Gonna) Love Me Again” from the Elton John biopic “Rocketman.”

Artistic Endeavors

In addition to his music career, Taupin is also an accomplished visual artist. His artwork, which includes paintings and sculptures, has been exhibited in galleries across the United States. Sales from his artwork also contribute to his net worth.

Charitable Work

Future Projects
