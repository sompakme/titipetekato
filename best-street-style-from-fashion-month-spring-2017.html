Best Street Style from Fashion Month Spring 2017

 Posted on July 24, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  533 words  |  Aldo Pusey
1

Olivia Palermo

It's always nice to catch Olivia when she doesn't have 20 paparazzi photographers hanging around her. 

2

Gilda Ambrosio

One of my favorite things when taking pictures is including some city elements—like the flower shops in Milan that frame Gilda's outfit so well. 

3

Yoyo Cao

Yoyo is one of my favorite bloggers to shoot. She is wearing another hero of the season, the Balenciaga platform boots, crossing the street as the tram approaches the 100s of photographers in Milan.

4

Hailey Baldwin

Shooting supermodels outside the shows can be insane and difficult. Kids wait for hours just to get a selfie with them, so it's a matter of waiting until the right moment arrives—which is 5 seconds max before she jumps in the car. Here is Hailey wearing D&G flowers from the runway. 

5

Irene Kim

It's so good to catch 'that moment', where the light and the movement are just right—brought to you by Irene Kim in NY. 

6

Sora Noi

This is the first time I was able to get photos from Marc Jacobs exits, as the show had always been night, when I was on my way to London already. One of my favorite pictures of the season is Sora Noi still wearing the dreadlocks from the show.

Vittoria Ceretti and Jess PW

Models off duty with two of my favorite new wave models—Vittoria Ceretti (right) and Jess PW (middle). Yasmin, you are also beautiful! 

8

Shea Marie and Caroline Vreeland

You can't have a fashion week without these two. BFFs Caroline V and Shea Marie bring some fun and color to the streets. 

9

Rihanna

When Riri shows up at the Dior show in Paris...you probably shoot 200 frames trying to get one picture.

10

Lexi Boling

The models formula to keep going during fashion week—champagne and cigarettes. 

11

Teddy

Shooting inside Musee Rodin in Paris with Teddy, why not?

Giovanna Battaglia

Justine Lee

Anna Della Russo

Yulia, Akimoto Kozue and Young Juvenile Youth

Evangelie Smyrniotaki

The Styleheroine's Evangelie, waiting for the show while matching this romantic Parisian lamp post with modern style. 

17

Chiara Ferragni

Shooting Chiara at Balmain was fun, and even more fun when paparazzi on the other side of the fence keep calling to you, saying 'white cap, move!'and  'white cap come here so I explain to you...' while you just focus on taking your picture. 

18

Sara Sampaio

Sara Sampaio was my favorite off-duty model to shoot outside the shows. These are the moments when street style photography feels just too good to be true. And then in the next few seconds, 20 photographers jump in front of you and the spell is broken.
