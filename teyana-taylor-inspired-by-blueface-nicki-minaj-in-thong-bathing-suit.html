It's crazy to think that a few months ago, nobody knew who Blueface was. Now, he's the owner of one of the most buzzworthy songs of the year. "Thotiana" has been remixed countless times and pretty much everybody has taken notice of the young Los Angeles rapper. During a recent trip to the beach, Teyana Taylor unleashed her own version of the song's lyrics, captioning one of her photos after Blueface's hook.

"Flow tailor-made like Teyana," wrote the G.O.O.D. Music artist, showing off her body and showing love to Nicki Minaj in the process. The lyrics are from Minaj's remix of Blueface's hit song, repurposing a few of the lines for her "Barbiana" drop. Teyana Taylor got comfortable on the beach in Miami, letting it all hang out in a leopard-print thong swimsuit. She flaunted her toned body and her tattoos while playing in the sand with her daughter. We're sure nobody was complaining about her sheer attire.
