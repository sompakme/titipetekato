Personal Life

Rowe’s romantic history includes two significant relationships. She first married Richard Edelman in 1982 and converted to Judaism.

However, their marriage ended in divorce in 1988. Rowe’s most high-profile relationship was with the legendary pop musician Michael Jackson.

The couple met while she was working as an assistant in Arnold Klein’s dermatology office, where Jackson was being treated for vitiligo.

They developed a deep connection, and Rowe proposed to bear Jackson’s children after divorcing Lisa Marie Presley in 1996. They married on November 14, 1996, in Sydney, Australia.

From their union, Rowe became the mother of two children. Their son, Michael Joseph Jackson Jr., was born on February 13, 1997, and their daughter, Paris Jackson, was born on April 3, 1998.

Although the couple divorced in April 2000, Rowe gave full custody rights of the children to Jackson. She decided to prioritize their well-being and entrusted their upbringing to Jackson.
